Key Questions

  • What are the current data collection and validation processes at the Bank of Tanzania?
  • How can an automated pipeline that integrates ETL and validation mechanisms be designed?
  • How effective is the automated pipeline in improving data quality at the Bank of Tanzania?
Scope 01

Datasets In Scope

Focused on internal BoT datasets: financial reports, macroeconomic indicators, and transactional data.

Scope 02

Evaluation Methods

Evaluation through mixed methods: quantitative (accuracy, completeness, consistency, timeliness) and qualitative workflow analysis.

Scope 03

Project Limits

Limited to technical and operational aspects of pipeline development; excludes broader institutional reforms or external governance.
