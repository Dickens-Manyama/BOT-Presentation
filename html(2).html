Project Overview
& Introduction

Context 01

Data Challenges at BoT

The Bank of Tanzania relies on manual and semi-manual processes (spreadsheets, emails, paper submissions). These methods are slow, error-prone, and reduce trust in financial data needed for policy and planning.

Context 02

Need for Automation

Manual handling introduces duplication, missing values, and inconsistencies. Automated pipelines improve efficiency, minimize human intervention, and strengthen data reliability for institutional decision-making.

Context 03

Academic & Practical Impact

This capstone project applies data science theory to real institutional problems. Automation enhances data quality, supports evidence-based policy, and builds student capacity in modern data engineering.
